Privacy Policy

Application: slicesonly  ·  Effective date: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how slicesonly (the "Application") handles data. The Application is a private, single-operator content-automation tool used by its owner (the "Operator") to publish original videos to the Operator's own TikTok account.

1. Who This Applies To

The Application has a single user: the Operator (its owner). It is not offered to the public and does not collect data from any other people.

2. Data the Application Uses

To publish to the Operator's own account, the Application stores the following locally on the Operator's own computer:

  • OAuth access and refresh tokens issued by TikTok when the Operator authorizes the Application. These let the Application post on the Operator's behalf.
  • The Operator's own generated video files and captions, created by the Application for posting.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

The Application does not collect, access, store, or process any data belonging to other TikTok users — no other accounts, content, followers, comments, or personal information. It does not use analytics or tracking, and it does not build user profiles.

4. How Data Is Stored

Tokens and content are stored locally on the Operator's own machine and are used solely to publish content to the Operator's own account. They are not transmitted to any third party other than TikTok's official API for the purpose of posting.

5. Data Sharing & Selling

The Application does not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. The only external service it communicates with is TikTok's official API, to post the Operator's own content.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

The Operator may delete the locally stored tokens and content at any time, and may revoke the Application's access through their TikTok account settings, which immediately ends the Application's ability to act on their behalf.

7. Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

8. Changes

This Policy may be updated from time to time; the effective date above will be revised accordingly.
